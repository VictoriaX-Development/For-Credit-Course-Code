<p>Hello everyone</p>
<p>Welcome to LLAMAS101 – Foundations of Llamatology.  Over the next 14 weeks, you'll join us for an in depth look at some of the most important features of llamas, and their lesser cousins, alpacas.</p>
<div style="border-radius: 1em 10em 10em 1em; box-sizing: border-box; background: #e7f0f3; padding: 1em; margin: 0 0 1em 1em; overflow: hidden; box-shadow: -.5em .5em 1em #eee;">
<h3>What are you going to learn?</h3>
<p><img src="https://blackboard.vuw.ac.nz/bbcswebdav/pid-2420170-dt-content-rid-7001560_1/xid-7001560_1" style="border-radius: 50%; border: solid .2em #166e8e; width: 8em; height: auto; background: #a6c8d4; margin: 0 3em 0 0; box-sizing: border-box; overflow: hidden; float: left;" alt="Cute llama" />By the end of the course, you'll be able to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Describe the important features of llamas</li>
<li>Contrast llamas with other animals</li>
<li>Explain why llamas are awesome</li>
<li>Present cool llama information to a chosen audience</li>
</ul>

Most of your interaction with your lecturers will online as well, mostly in the discussion boards. If you're ever in Wellington, you're  also welcome to talk to us in person.</p>
